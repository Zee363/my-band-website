Get to know a little about the band members!

Luphindo Ngxanga

Luphindo Ngxanga is a South African vocalist, singer, song-writer, professional photographer, event coordinator, and member of South African award-winning music group, The Soil. Luphindo is also the brother of Ntsika Ngxanga, also a member of The Soil.

Ntsika Ngxanga

Ntsika Fana Ngxanga, widely known as Ntsika, is a South African singer, songwriter, theatre music director, soloist, and member of platinum selling South African music group, The Soil. Ntsika is also a producer and the record label head of Nomadic Tribe Entertainment. His interest in music began while he was in primary school and later got introduced to the apostolic gospel-style of singing. Ntsika, a passionate and gifted singer, is said to receive inspiration for his songs through visions and in his dreams.

Buhle Mda

Buhlebendalo Mda, professionally known as Buhle Mda, is a South African artiste, visionary, gender activist, and former member of award-winning and platinum selling music group, The Soil. Aside from her singing prowess, Buhle Mda has an impressionable personality, thus drawing a cult-like following for herself. She is also explored solo career even as a member of the group, The Soil.

Theo Matshoba

Theo Matshoba is a vocalist, a member of the The Soil who replaced Buhle in the group. She is from Dobsonville and is not from a musical family. Theo says she learned music the kasi way, from the streets and we see her today blossoming from that knowledge.
